# Security Policy

The GoGetCash team takes the security of our software and user data seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and we will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

## Supported Versions

Only the latest version of GoGetCash available on the Google Play Store is actively supported with security updates. We encourage all users to keep their app updated to ensure they have the latest security patches and features.

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| Latest  | :white_check_mark: |
| < 1.0   | :x:                |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We encourage you to report any potential security vulnerabilities to us privately. Publicly disclosing a vulnerability could put other users at risk, so we urge you to follow this process.

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.**

Instead, please email your findings to:

**`security@gogetcash.com`** (or your preferred private email address)

### What to Include

To help us investigate and resolve the issue quickly, please provide as much of the following information as possible:

*   **Type of vulnerability** (e.g., Cross-Site Scripting, Insecure Data Storage, Information Exposure, etc.).
*   **A detailed description** of the vulnerability and the potential impact.
*   **Step-by-step instructions** on how to reproduce the issue.
*   **Proof-of-concept or exploit code**, if applicable.
*   **Any relevant screenshots or video recordings.**
*   **The version of the app** you were using and the Android version of your device.

### Our Commitment

If you report a vulnerability, we commit to the following:

*   We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **3 business days**.
*   We will provide a more detailed response within **7 business days**, including our initial assessment and a timeline for a fix if required.
*   We will keep you informed of our progress as we work to resolve the issue.
*   We will publicly credit you for your discovery (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).

Thank you for helping keep GoGetCash and our users safe.
